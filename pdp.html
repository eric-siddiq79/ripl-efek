HOW TO USE

For sustainable and best practices, hand wash use 1/2 -1 oz small load, 1-3 oz medium load, sheets, towels, etc. use 2-5 oz. Add more if you want a more fragrant wash.

packaging

Our veseel and refill cans are made from Aluminum. They're meant to be refilled but whenever you're done with them, they are 100% recyclable.

SUBSCRIPTION CALCULATOR

We've worked out some averages to help you decide. One refill makes a 16 fl. oz. can of laundry detergent. That's about 45 loads per refill. Let's assume one person washes their laundry once a week. That refill will last 33 days, about 1 month.
