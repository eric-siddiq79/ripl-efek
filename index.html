OUR STORY
Founded by Shawnique Alexander, Ripl Efek looks at how we wash our clothes affect our body, our earth and how it is purchased.

“Looking at my life, I noticed that I was just being cluttered with unnecessary products, junk, and toxic waste. As I observed my bathroom, my kitchen, my laundry room, I realized I was surrounded by silent killers that are contaminating our MOTHER EARTH, starting with my home and I felt a strong calling to help eliminate it all– starting with the clothes that my family put on their body every day. What started as a consignment, second hand store concept evolved quickly into identifying why clothes were so disposable in the first place. This desire to want more from our belongings and in return for the earth we belong in, was the key reason to starting the Ripl Efek journey. 

I hope you enjoy this journey with me, let’s move gently and powerfully together.”
